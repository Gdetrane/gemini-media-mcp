# Image Generation Style Reference

## Photography Styles

### Camera & Lens
- **Portrait**: 85mm f/1.8, shallow depth of field, bokeh background
- **Landscape**: 24mm wide-angle, deep focus f/16, panoramic
- **Macro**: macro lens, extreme close-up, shallow DOF
- **Street**: 35mm prime, candid framing, natural light
- **Action**: fast shutter, GoPro wide-angle, motion blur
- **Fashion editorial**: medium format Hasselblad, studio lighting

### Camera Brands (affect color science)
- **Fujifilm**: warm, film-like color science, great for portraits
- **Leica**: contrasty, clinical sharpness
- **GoPro**: ultra-wide distortion, immersive action feel
- **Disposable camera**: raw, nostalgic flash aesthetic, grain
- **Polaroid**: instant film colors, soft focus, white border

### Film Stocks
- **Kodak Portra 400**: warm skin tones, pastel palette
- **Kodak Ektar**: vivid saturated colors, fine grain
- **Fujifilm Velvia**: ultra-saturated landscapes
- **Ilford HP5**: classic black and white, beautiful grain
- **CineStill 800T**: cinematic tungsten, halation glow

## Lighting Vocabulary

### Studio
- Three-point softbox setup
- Ring light (even, shadowless)
- Rembrandt lighting (triangle shadow on cheek)
- Butterfly lighting (glamour, beauty)
- Split lighting (dramatic, half-face shadow)

### Natural
- Golden hour (warm, long shadows, 1hr before sunset)
- Blue hour (cool tones, twilight)
- Overcast diffused (soft, even, no harsh shadows)
- Harsh midday sun (high contrast, sharp shadows)
- Window light (soft directional, Vermeer-like)

### Dramatic
- Chiaroscuro (extreme light/dark contrast)
- Backlighting / rim lighting (subject silhouette outline)
- Volumetric fog / light rays (god rays, atmosphere)
- Neon glow (cyberpunk, night scenes)
- Candlelight (warm, intimate, flickering)

## Prompt Templates by Use Case

### Photorealistic Scene
```
A [subject] [action] in [location]. Shot on [camera/lens], [lighting].
[Atmosphere/mood]. [Additional details about textures, colors, environment].
```

### Product Mockup
```
[Product] on [surface material], [lighting setup (e.g. three-point softbox)],
[camera angle (e.g. elevated 45-degree angle)], sharp focus on [key detail],
[background treatment]. [Aspect ratio for intended platform].
```

### Illustration / Sticker
```
[Style (e.g. kawaii-style, flat vector)] illustration of [subject],
[outline style (bold/thin/none)], [shading approach (cel-shaded/gradient/flat)],
[color palette], [background (white/transparent/colored)].
```

### Text-in-Image (Logo, Sign, Menu)
```
[Image type] with text "[exact wording]" in [font style (e.g. bold sans-serif)].
[Design approach]. [Color scheme]. [Layout description].
```
Note: Keep text to 25 characters or fewer for best results. Use Pro model for text rendering.
